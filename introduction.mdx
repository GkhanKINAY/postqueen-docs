PostQueen is an AI social media assistant. She writes the copy, designs the visuals and schedules everything across 30+ networks, and your job is to review the calendar. She runs on your terms: on autopilot, Temporal publishes every scheduled post on time, and if you ask for drafts she holds each one until you approve it.

## Talk to her anywhere

She answers in ChatGPT and the Claude app, reaches WhatsApp and Telegram through OpenClaw or Hermes, and is one command away in your terminal. The mobile app is on the way. If your assistant can run a CLI command or call MCP, it can run your socials, and the [agents overview](/agents/overview) shows every path.
