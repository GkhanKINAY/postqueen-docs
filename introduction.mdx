## What is PostQueen?

PostQueen is the queen of your posts: an AI social media assistant that writes the copy, designs the visuals and schedules it all across 30+ networks. Tell her what you want the world to see, in one sentence. She handles the rest, and you just review the calendar.

- **One calendar, 30+ networks.** Write a post once and she reshapes it for each platform's limits and habits, then publishes on the schedule you set.
- **She writes, designs and films.** Give her a topic and she comes back with a hook, a caption in your voice and a matching image. Ask and she adds a short AI-voiced video.
- **She keeps score.** Follower growth and per-post engagement live beside your calendar, and when a post crosses the like milestone you set, she can repost it or add a follow-up comment.

**She runs on your terms.** Leave her alone and she is a true autopilot: whatever lands on the calendar goes out on time, published by a Temporal workflow that retries on failure, without another word from you. Want more say? Ask for drafts and she holds each post until you approve it.

## Talk to her anywhere

She is not locked inside a dashboard. She answers in ChatGPT and the Claude app, reads your WhatsApp and Telegram messages through OpenClaw or Hermes, and is one command away in your terminal. The mobile app is on the way, but she already answers on your phone: through OpenClaw or Hermes, or at [app.postqueen.ai](https://app.postqueen.ai). If your assistant can run a CLI command or call MCP, it can run your socials. Start with the [agents overview](/agents/overview).
