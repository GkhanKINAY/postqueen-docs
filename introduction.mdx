## What is PostQueen?

PostQueen is the queen of your posts: your AI assistant that writes the posts, makes the images, edits the videos, and schedules them on your calendar for your approval. You say the word, she does the rest, and nothing goes out until you approve it.

- **Schedule everywhere.** Plan and publish to 30+ social networks from one visual calendar.
- **Generate with AI.** Write posts in your voice, design images, and turn a photo and a script into short videos.
- **Talk to your AI agent.** Draft and schedule in plain English from Claude Code, ChatGPT, Cursor, OpenClaw and more, over the CLI or MCP server.
