## What is PostQueen?

PostQueen is an open-source, AI-native platform for managing all your social media accounts from one place. Plan, generate, and publish across **30+ networks (34 integrations)** — with AI agents, analytics, and team collaboration built in.

- Schedule social media posts and long-form articles from a single visual calendar
- Generate captions, images, and short videos with AI
- Automate everything through the Public API, CLI, NodeJS SDK, n8n node, and MCP server
