PostQueen is an AI employee for your social media. Tell her what to share, in one sentence. She writes the copy, designs the visual and schedules it on every channel you have. You just review the calendar.

**She runs on your terms.** Leave her alone and she is a true autopilot: whatever lands on the calendar goes out on time without another word from you. Want more say? Everything is visible before it publishes, so you can edit or delete anything, and if you ask for drafts she holds every post until you approve it.
