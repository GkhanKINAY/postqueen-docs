---
title: 'Meet PostQueen'
description: 'The queen of your posts: an AI social media assistant that writes, designs and schedules across 30+ networks while you review the calendar.'
---

PostQueen drafts the copy, makes the visual and puts it on the calendar. You open the calendar, look at the week and change anything you do not like. She runs on your terms: on autopilot she publishes your scheduled posts on time, and if you ask for drafts she holds each one until you approve it.

## Talk to her anywhere

She answers in ChatGPT and the Claude app, reaches WhatsApp and Telegram through OpenClaw or Hermes, and is one command away in your terminal. The mobile app is on the way.
